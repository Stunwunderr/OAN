Who we are

ONEL is an agro-commodity trading company with a primary focus on sourcing and exportation of raw and proceed agricultural products from Nigeria to any parts of the globe. Some of our exportable agricultural commodities are bitter kola, shea butter, cashew nut, kola nut, cocoa bean, ginger

OAN Nature Export Limited (ONEL) is a Limited Liability Company incorporated with the country's Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). We are licensed non oil export company based in Lagos Nigeria. We serve clients from diverse parts of the world.

  • At ONEL, we understand what customer needs is and committed to supplying high value products.
  • Our Customer satisfaction is our priority and vital us, so we do our best and strive to exceed our customers expectations.
  • Our aim is to build a strong brand name and develop long-term relationships with our customers by providing solutions to their demands.

It is operating from its head office located in Ikorodu, Lagos (Nigeria).
